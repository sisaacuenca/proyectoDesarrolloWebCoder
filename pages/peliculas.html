STAR WARS- FAN PAGE

Movies

  • Star Wars: A New Hope(1977)

    Luke Skywalker embarks on a journey that will change the galaxy. Nineteen years after the formation of the empire, luke is thrust into the fight of the rebel alliance when he meets obi-wan kenobi, who has lived in isolation for years on the desert planet of taooine. Obi-Wan trains Luke as Jedi when he joins him on a dangerous mission to rescue the beautiful Princess Leia from the clutches of Darth Vader and the empire of evil.

  • Star Wars: Empire Strikes Back(1980)

    After the destruction of the Death Star, the Imperial forces continue to pursue the rebels. Following the defeat of the rebellion on the ice planet Hoth, Luke travels to the planet Dagobah to train with Jedi Master Yoda. In an attempt to turn Luke to the dark side, Darth Vader lures young Skywalker into a trap in Bespin's cloud city

  • Star Wars: Return Of The Jedi(1983)

    The empire prepares to crush the Rebellion with a more powerful death star, while the Rebel fleet mounts a massive attack on the space station. Luke Skywalker faces Darth Vader in a climactic final duel against the evil Emperor.

  • Star Wars: The Phantom Menace(1999)

    Obi-wan kenobi and his Jedi master Qui-Gon Jinn discover that Anakin gains the freedom to leave his home and be trained as a Jedi. Anakin and the Queen face invasion forces, while the two Jedi compete against Darth Maul. But the invasion is the first step in a plan for the reemerging forces of darkness, the Sith.

  • Star Wars: Attack Of The Clones(2002)

    10 years after the invasion of Naboo, the galaxy is on the brink of civil war. Under the leadership of a renegade Jedi named Count Dooku, thousands of solar systems threaten to secede from the Galactic Republic. When an attempt is made to assassinate Senator Padmé Amidala, Jedi apprentice Anakin Skywalker is assigned to protect her. During his mission, anakin discovers his love for Padmé, as well as his own dark side. soon, Anakin, Padmé and Obi-wan kenobi are drawn to the heart of the separatist movement and the start of the clone war

  • Star Wars: Revenge Of The Sith(2005)

    Years after the start of the Clone Wars, Jedi Knights lead a huge army of clones. When the sinister Sith reveal a plot to rule the galaxy, the republic collapses and from its ashes rises the evil galactic empire. Jedi hero Anakin Skywalker, is seduced by the dark side of the force to become the apprentice of Emperor Darth Vader.

  • Star Wars: The Force Awakens(2015)

    As Kylo Ren and the sinister First Order rise from the ashes of the Empire, Luke Skywalker disappears when the galaxy needs him most. Rey, a desert scavenger, and Finn, a deserted stormtrooper, are asked to join forces with Han Solo and Chewbacca in their desperate search for the only hope to restore peace to the galaxy.

  • Rogue One: A Star Wars Story(2016)

    In a time of conflict, a group of unlikely heroes band together on a suicide mission to steal the plans for "The Death Star," the Empire's ultimate weapon of destruction.

  • Star Wars: The Last Jedi(2017)

    The resistance tries to escape from their base on the planet D'Qar.- While Rey tries to convince Luke Skywalker to train her in the ways of the force, Finn and Rose Tico try to Sabotage the First Order fleet so that the surviving members from the resistance can escapes

  • Solo: A Star Wars Story(2018)

    Young Han Solo and his gang of smugglers intend to steal coaxium from the planet Kessel. Since he needs a very fast ship for the mission, Solo contacts Lando Calrissian, the owner of the Millennium Falcon.

  • Star Wars: The Rise Of Skywalker(2019)

    A year after the events of "The Last Jedi", the remnants of the Resistance will once again face the First Order, involving conflicts of the past and present. Meanwhile, the ancient conflict between the Jedi and the Sith will reach its climax, bringing the Skywalker saga to a definitive end.
